Frequently Asked Questions

Real Estate Investing

Why use Invest by Eureka?

Invest is the latest investor platform for Eureka Investment Fund. Built by tech, fintech and real estate tech professionals, Invest allows any real estate investor, beginner or veteran, to successfully invest with Eureka Fund in California real estate. For more information and details on how you’ll benefit from investing with Eureka Fund, please visit our Apple store app page and reach out to our investment managers!

Why should I invest with the real estate investment managers at Eureka Investment Fund?

Life is busy enough with work, family and personal responsibilities taking up most of your time. Let the experts at Eureka Investment Fund take care of investing in real estate for you. Eureka’s investment managers provide end to end service, including syndicating and pooling capital, performing due diligence, construction management and much more. All you have to do is -deposit your investment capital, sit back and relax.

What sets Eureka Investment Fund apart from its competitors?

Eureka Investment Funds’ emphasis on integrating technology and operations and using veteran development teams allows us to continue delivering consistent returns and stress free investing for our clients.

Why does Eureka only invest in development and rehab projects?

Eureka Fund is currently invested in only residential development projects because of our focus in California markets. Investment margins for rental investments have only been slightly better than break even in California’s competitive markets so we’ve focused only on higher margin development and rehab projects. We are branching out into other states for possible rental opportunities (explanation below).

Does Eureka invest in out of state properties?

Not at the moment. Eureka Fund is currently invested in California Bay Area residential development projects but we anticipate out of state investments in the coming 2-3 years, at the latest. Our investment managers are exploring partnership opportunities with other wealth managers partners in the industry. Stay tuned and we’ll be posting more information in the coming months on out of state investment opportunities.

Who are the investment managers for Eureka Investment Fund?

Eureka Investment Fund is run by a veteran team of real estate investors and advisors who are experts in real estate, lending and asset management and more. This combined multi-decades of team experience helps our investors achieve industry competitive returns currently in California and we plan on taking those talents into new markets in and out of state.

Investor

What is the expected return as an investor in Eureka Investment Fund?

2-3x equity multiple. 25-30% annualized leveraged internal rate of return.

Is there a minimum investment amount?

1 investment share is $50,000 minimum. There is no maximum but investors generally look to invest between $50,000 - 250,000.

How many investors are in Eureka Fund?

This number varies as we work with different investors all throughout the world. We’ll have investor demographic studies in the near future so stay tuned for more details on who’s investing in the real estate industry.

Can I invest in Eureka Investment Fund if I don’t live in California?

Yes, Eureka Fund works with our private network of investors and if interested, we can have our investment managers reach out to assess appropriate fit of investors for our investment funds.

How long do I have to be invested in Eureka Investment Fund?

Investing in Eureka Investment Fund generally lasts for minimum of one year but we allow the flexibility of shorter terms where investors can exit at the end of each investment project.

Does Eureka Investment Fund provide dividends?

No, Eureka Investment Fund currently only provides liquidity events by selling or purchasing shares. We don’t provide dividends but are exploring a reentry into rental investment markets where dividend payouts would be appropriate.

What are the requirements for being a Eureka Investment Fund investor?

The requirements for being a Eureka Investment Fund investor are the standard prerequisites for a high net worth individual investments. An investor is typically required to earn gross income greater than $250,000 annually or have assets (excluding primary residence) of greater than $1,000,000 in assets. If an investor doesn’t qualify as a high net worth individual, there are other more restrictive alternatives including qualifying as a knowledgeable domain or industry professional. Please reach out to us if there’s any questions regarding these rules and one of our investment associates will be happy to assist you.

Legal and Tax

Do I need a professional (lawyer, account, etc.) to help manage my investments?

Investing in Eureka Investment Fund doesn’t require a tax or legal professional but the investment managers strongly recommend that you have representation or have consulted with both experts from both fields of profession before investing with the Fund. Please reach out to our investment managers if referrals are needed.

Does Eureka Investment Fund provide tax reporting documents for investors?

Yes, Eureka’s investment team provides all year end tax reporting paperwork and documentation necessary for our investors to properly file their annual tax liabilities.

Is Eureka Investment Fund structured as a partnership or corporation?

Eureka Investment Funds are structured as CA Limited Liability Corporations and Funds within the series are not legally associated with each other through a consolidated holding company.

Is investing in Eureka Investment Fund considered a short term or long term investment?

Investing with Eureka Investment Fund is considered a short term capital gains event. The investment team is preparing for long term capital gains investments in the near future with out of state rental investment opportunities. We will have more information for our investors and clients in the coming weeks.
